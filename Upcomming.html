1] Tata Altroz Racer:

Expected to launch in March 2024, this sporty variant of the Altroz hatchback aims to deliver an exhilarating driving experience at an estimated price of ₹10.00 lakh


2] Tata Curvv EV:

Set to hit the roads in July 2024, the Curvv EV is an electric vehicle with futuristic styling and a projected price of ₹20.00 lakh


3] Tata Curvv:

Also arriving in August 2024, the non-electric version of the Curvv promises a blend of elegance and performance, priced around ₹10.50 lakh


4] Tata Avinya:

Anticipated to launch in January 2025, the Avinya is a premium offering with an estimated price of ₹30.00 lakh


5] Tata Harrier EV:

Scheduled for an April 2025 debut, the Harrier EV combines Tata’s SUV prowess with electric mobility, priced at approximately ₹30.00 lakh


6] Tata Punch 2025:

Expected in June 2025, the Punch 2025 is a compact SUV designed for urban adventures, with an affordable price tag of ₹6.00 lakh


7] Tata Sierra:

Making a comeback in December 2025, the Sierra is an iconic SUV with modern features, priced around ₹25.00 lakh


8] Tata Atmos:

Another mystery awaits us with the Atmos, set to launch at an estimated price of ₹12.00 lakh


9] Tata Kite 5:

Details about this compact sedan are yet to be revealed, but it’s expected to be priced at ₹4.50 lakh
